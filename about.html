About CC





    At Capital Cubing we aim at better competitions and better interaction of our cubing community through cube meets. This is for everybody who wanted to organise a competition but could not because of guidance problems or lack of an organising team. This is for everyone who could see mistakes in competitions and wanted to fix them. We provide you that platform and resources that you need.



    If you've been solving that Rubik's Cube for a while, and would like to compete, take a look at the upcoming competitions. If you register for an account, you can find other speedcubers in your area. If you're new to competing, see our Competitor Tutorial so you know what's in store for you when you go to a competition.
