Register



Fee Structure

  1. A base fees of INR 150 and additional fees of INR 25 per event is applicable. For example, if you do 4 events, your fees is 150 + (4 x 25 )= INR 250.



Steps to Register

  1. 1. Register on the WCA website. (LINK)
  2. Pay the registration fee through one of the below options:
    a. Calculate your payment amount and transfer through Paytm to 8447852838.
    In the description, mention NCR Open 2017, your name and WCA ID(if any).
    b. Pay through Instamojo (LINK)
  3. Wait upto 48 hours for your name to appear in list of registered participants. (LINK)
  4. Registration closes on 16th June, 2017.



On the Spot Registration

  1. This will be available from 8:00 to 8:30 A.M. on 23 June and 24 June.
  2. A base fees of INR 250 and additional fees of INR 25 per event is applicable. (i.e. INR 100 more than online fees)
