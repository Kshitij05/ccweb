Travel






By Metro

The nearest metro station to the venue is Samaypur Badli on yellow line. You can either take a reserved auto rickshaw to the competition venue or a shared auto rickshaw/ E-rickshaw which will drop you at the main gate of college.

By Air

You can get many cab services from Delhi Airport such as Uber/Ola etc. which will drop you to the venue directly.

From Main Gate

  1. Enter the main gate and go straight to reach kalpana chawla girls hostel.
  2. Take a left from there. Walk straight till you reach a T point. Then take the right.
  3. Walk straight and you will reach a four way. Go straight and civil block will be on your left in which there is the venue.
  4. Ask any student about convocation hall or mechanical canteen in case of any confusion.
