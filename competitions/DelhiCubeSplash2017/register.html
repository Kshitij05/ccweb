Register



Fee Structure

A base fees of INR 200 and additional fees of INR 50 per event is applicable. For example, if you do 4 events, your fees is 200 + (4 x 50 )= INR 400



Steps to Register

  1. Register on the WCA website. (LINK)
  2. Pay the registration fee through one of the below options:
    a. Calculate your payment amount and transfer through Paytm to 8447852838.
    In the description, mention Delhi Cube Splash 2017, your name and WCA ID(if any).
    b. Pay through Instamojo (LINK)
  3. Wait upto 48 hours for your name to appear in list of registered participants. (LINK)
  4. Registration closes on 24th July 2017, 23:59 IST.



Note

No on the spot registration is available. Online registration is mandatory.
