Travel






By Metro

The nearest metro station to the venue is Govind Puri on the purple line which is approximately a kilometer away and you can take a rickshaw/auto/walk to the venue.

By Air

You can get many cab services from Delhi Airport such as Uber/Ola etc. which will drop you to the venue directly.
