WANT TO KNOW ABOUT US?

Gan Cube is the manufacturer of very high quality speedcubes. Over the past few years, they have taken the speedcube market by storm. Both the current and previous world record for Rubik's Cube have been set on a variant of Gan Air. The Gan Air comes with a groundbreaking design. Usually, a cube being lighter meant it will also be slower. With the invention of Gan Air, that is no longer true. Weighing just 73 grams, the puzzle still boasts of unprecedented stability and speed. The puzzle also has a 55 Degrees corner cutting and 30 degrees reverse corner cutting along with circuilar center cap design which ensures minimal lockups when solving. Their flagship product - 'Gan Air UM' combines the strength of Gan Air with a magnetic positioning system. All these cubes are handmade and each cube takes over 3 hours to be prepared by a highly skilled technician. This is also the world's first mass produced magnetic cube. The Gan Air UM also comes with a different variety of springs with varying elasticity so each cuber can get the feel of the puzzle that best suits the cuber.
